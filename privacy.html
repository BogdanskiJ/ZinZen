How ZinZen protects (personal) data:
We minimally abide by the laws of the countries we operate in - and we only operate in countries that have acceptable laws.
We go further by applying the strong(est) General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) - even when not required.
We go even further by applying any other reasonable privacy or security measures.

By default, ZinZen promises:
- Login options: no login / anonymous / email (alias) / social).
- ALL your personal data is safe and private - FOREVER.
- You don't get ANY dark patterns or advertisements - EVER.
- ANYTHING you choose to send over the internet to us is protected and encrypted - in transit and at rest.
- You don't get ANY cookies. If you choose to login you get a cookie that only stores an anonymous access token.

Storage on your device
Virtually all apps store data on your device without encrypting it. Even the most secure messaging apps don't have an extra password lock. They rely on your phone protection.
For the truly paranoid we'll provide an option to password-protect ZinZen at the app-level.

What (personal) data ZinZen collects - and if/why we use it:
"Personal data" is any data about you or your behavior that can be traced back to you without disproportionate effort.
The rest is just "data".
By default, all personal data is stored FOR YOUR EYES ONLY.
For ZinZen to share any personal data - any personal data at all - YOU need to ask us EXPLICITLY, even when you want to share your data with us, ZinZen.
Only then will ZinZen share what you chose to share - and only that - to only those you chose to share with.
If you choose to share your personal data with ZinZen by clicking the button 'share with ZinZen anonymously' - any connection to you as a person will be removed; turning it into data.
This data helps ZinZen improve your experience and that of others, as well as develop new services.

To update/manage/export/delete your personal data : ZinZen => My settings.
