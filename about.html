ZinZen lets you plan your drive - so you can enjoy your life. A purpose planner.
Create your best authentic self - with others. But also keep your data private by default!
Individuals, teams and institutions collaborate to self-direct and self-organize on ZinZen.

How does ZinZen work?
1. Create goals for yourself, just like with any other todo-app. Swipe/right-click to edit. Tap/click for sub-goals.
2. ZinZen then:
- Helps turn big life goals into simple tasks and habits with crowd-sourced suggestions.
- Plans your calendar automagically.
- Connects you with others on shared or matching goals.
- Encourages and alerts you when promises are at risk.
Ultimately, ZinZen helps you create the future you want, while allowing you to relax and fully enjoy every moment of the present.

Who owns ZinZen?
ZinZen will be steward-owned. This means a legal enforcement that:
1. All decisions are made by the stewards - and the stewards only.
2. All decisions must be in the best interests of the company's purpose.
Consequences:
- ZinZen's decision power can never be sold or inherited.
- All net profits go to making ZinZen better.
NB: Investment rounds (crowdfunding or other) are capped.
Once a predetermined (fair) % is reached - the round ends.
